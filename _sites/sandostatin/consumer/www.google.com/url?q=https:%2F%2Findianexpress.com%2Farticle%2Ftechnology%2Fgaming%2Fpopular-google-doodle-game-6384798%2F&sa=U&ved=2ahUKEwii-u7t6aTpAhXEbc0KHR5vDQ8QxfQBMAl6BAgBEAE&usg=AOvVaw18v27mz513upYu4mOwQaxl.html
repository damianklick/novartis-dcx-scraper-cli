Popular Google Doodle Games: How to play with the Fischinger doodle

Popular Google Doodle Games:¬†The Fischinger doodle lets you create your own music. Here's how you can create your own tunes with it.The Fischinger doodle was originally posted back in 2017 to pay homage to influential filmmaker and visual artist, Oskar Fischinger on his 117th birthday.

By: Tech Desk | New Delhi | Updated: May 4, 2020 12:39:03 pm
The Fischinger doodle was originally posted back in 2017 to pay homage to influential filmmaker and visual artist, Oskar Fischinger on his 117th birthday.

Popular Google Doodle Games: Google is bringing back some of the most popular Doodle games from the past for users to stay entertained while they are at home self quarantining. To play with these doodles, you can simply head over to google.com and click on the interactive Google logo. This will take you to that day’s doodle. The company is calling this the ‘Stay and Play at Home with Popular Past Google Doodles’ initiative.

The company’s third doodle under its ‘Stay and Play at Home with Popular Past Google Doodles’ initiative was the Fischinger doodle. With it, users can create music tones from four different instruments using your PC mouse. Inside of the doodle, you can select a few dots on the screen. Doing so you can create fun and quirky tones and send them to your friends or listen/download them on your phone.

The Fischinger doodle was originally posted back in 2017 to pay homage to influential filmmaker and visual artist, Oskar Fischinger on his 117th birthday.

Google has a huge inventory of doodles and the company is picking these special doodles from there. These are not new ones. Google says the doodles that it will showcase through the upcoming days will be among the best and popular ones the company has ever created.

How to play with the Fischinger doodle

* To play this game, open a new tab on your Google Chrome browser or visit Google.com.

* Click on the doodle image that has the Google logo integrated into it.

* This will take you to a results page with the doodle game as the top result.
